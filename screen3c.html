👤 FOUNDER / PROPHET

MUHAMMAD IBN ABDULLAH (570–632 CE) — The Seal of the Prophets.

Unlike founders who were purely spiritual teachers or later institutional formalizers, Muhammad was simultaneously a religious visionary, a political leader, and a military commander. He did not claim to found a NEW religion, but to RESTORE the original monotheism of Abraham (*Ibrahim*).

⚠️ RELIGARE OBSERVATION: Muhammad represents the complete synthesis of spiritual and temporal authority within a single lifetime. This makes Islam the most "functional" and "integrated" of the major traditions from its point of origin.
📖 SACRED TEXT

THE QURAN — The direct, literal word of God revealed to Muhammad through the Angel Gabriel (*Jibril*).

THE HADITH: The recorded sayings and actions of the Prophet, which provide the 'Sunnah' (example) for living. The Quran is the Law; the Hadith is the manual for its application.

CRITICAL FACT: The Quran is considered untranslatable. It exists truly only in Arabic. The rhythm, sound, and structure are part of its divinity. For the Muslim, the Quran is not just a book *about* God; it is the presence of God made manifest in speech.

🌌 CREATION MYTH / COSMOLOGY

TAWHID — The Oneness of God.

God (Allah) is absolutely singular, beyond image, and the sole creator of the universe. Unlike Catholicism's Trinity, Islam's cosmology is strictly non-dual in its source of authority.

  • Universe created by divine command: "Be!" (*Kun*) and it is.
  • Creation is a 'Sign' (*Ayah*) pointing back to the Creator.
  • Humanity's purpose: To be God's *Khalifa* (Steward) on Earth.
⚖️ CORE BELIEF SYSTEM
1. Shahada (Faith), 2. Salat (Prayer), 3. Zakat (Charity), 4. Sawm (Fasting), 5. Hajj (Pilgrimage). These are the functional requirements of the faith.
Unlike the "Original Sin" of Catholicism, Islam posits *Fitra* — the belief that every human is born naturally inclined toward the good and the recognition of God. Evil is not an inherited stain, but a result of 'Ghaflah' (forgetfulness) and environmental deviation.
The mystical dimension of Islam focused on the direct experience of God's presence. Figures like Rumi and Ibn Arabi explored the 'Polishing of the Heart' to mirror divine light.
🎯 FUNCTIONAL PURPOSE

RESTORATION OF ORDER: Islam unified the fragmented tribes of Arabia under a single law (*Sharia*) and a single community (*Ummah*).

CULTURAL PRESERVATION: During the European Dark Ages, Islamic scholars translated and preserved Greek and Persian philosophy, mathematics, and medicine, seeding the later European Renaissance.

💰 POWER STRUCTURE

Islam does not have a formal priesthood. Authority is decentralized but guided by the *Ulama* (scholars of law). The primary split is between:

  • SUNNI: Leadership based on consensus and community choice.
  • SHIA: Leadership based on the lineage of the Prophet (Ali and the Imams).
