CATHOLICISM
THE ONE I KNOW
Founded: ~30 AD (spiritual) / 325 AD (institutional)
Origin: Jerusalem → Rome
Sacred Text: The Holy Bible
Practitioners Today: 1.3 billion

The world's largest religious institution. But did you know its doctrine was finalized at a political meeting in 325 AD by a Roman Emperor who needed to unify his empire?

🔥
ZOROASTRIANISM
THE ONE I KNOW NOTHING ABOUT
Founded: ~1500–600 BCE
Origin: Ancient Persia / Iran
Sacred Text: The Avesta (the Gathas)
Practitioners Today: ~120,000

The world's first organized religion. Almost nobody has heard of it. Yet it invented angels, Satan, the Messiah, and the apocalypse — all ideas Christianity and Islam later adopted.

🌹
ROSICRUCIANISM
THE ONE I'M CURIOUS ABOUT
Founded: 1614 AD (three manifestos)
Origin: Protestant Germany
Sacred Text: Fama Fraternitatis (1614)
Status: Secret Brotherhood — may never have existed

In 1614, three anonymous pamphlets appeared in Germany describing a secret Brotherhood with ancient wisdom. No member ever came forward. The Brotherhood may be fictional. The ideas changed the world anyway.
