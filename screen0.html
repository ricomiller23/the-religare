THE RELIGARE
A Telescope for Belief Systems
In 1610, Galileo built a wooden tube and said: everything you believe is wrong — and I can prove it. Just look.

The Catholic Church refused to look through the telescope. It took them 300 years to admit he was right.

The Religare is your telescope. It has no opinions. No answers. It is only a framework for gathering facts — a tool that lets you see past the cultural veil into the true nature of things.

You are about to study three religions: one you know, one you know nothing about, and one you're curious about.
